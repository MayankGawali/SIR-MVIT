Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of technology

Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology (Sir MVIT) is a well-regarded engineering college in Bangalore, Karnataka, established in 1986 by the Sri Krishnadevaraya Educational Trust, named after India's famous engineer, Sir M. Visvesvaraya; it's affiliated with VTU, known for its sprawling campus, strong infrastructure, diverse UG/PG engineering & management programs, research focus, and notable placements, holding an 'A' grade accreditation from NAAC
